ALIMI COFFEE TABLE

Editorial

ALIMI Coffee Table Layout is a visual exploration that merges African rhythm with modern minimalism. This project reimagines the texture and tone of traditional jazz visuals through a contemporary lens — celebrating improvisation, texture, and the elegance of black and white space.

“Design is the silent rhythm between image, text, and white space.”
