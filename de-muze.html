DE MUZE

Posters

De Muze, a poster inspired by my first experience at the De Muze Jazz Cafe. This poster emulates how it felt listening to Mazzle play their different sets. As I closed my eyes and opened them again, the view hit me; the blur mixed with the music, making the experience feel complete. Each feature you see on this poster tells a story of the night.


The colour used on this poster emulates the dim-coloured lights of the scene, the blur used to imitate my exact view upon opening my eyes and the composition of the text was to present the night how a jazz bar would, in a legible way.
