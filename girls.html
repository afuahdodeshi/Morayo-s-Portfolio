GIRLS.

Posters

GIRLS., inspired by Nancy Honey and her piece Entering the Masquerade at MoMu. The way the pictures were put together in a grid motivated me to make a poster in the same dimensions, therefore the use of Swiss Design.

The big text element, "GIRLS.," was to emphasize the function of the poster, showcasing the overall exhibition. The use of white space and aligned text imitates the Swiss grids.
